---
title: "Dashboard overview and navigation"
description: "The VEN 911 dashboard is your role-specific command center, showing recent news, system notices, and metrics based on your assigned role in the system."
---

When you log in to Portal VEN 911, you land at `/dashboard` — a home screen tailored to your role. Every user sees their name and role displayed at the top, a feed of recent news articles, and a system notices panel with reminders relevant to their work. Administrators also see a live metrics summary for the entire system.

## Welcome header

The dashboard greets you by name and displays your assigned role in red:

> **Bienvenido(a), [your name]**
> Estás accediendo bajo el rol de: **[your role]**

This header appears on every page load and reflects the account your administrator created for you. You cannot change your own role — only an Administrador can update it.

## Recent news feed

The left panel shows the three most recent published news articles, ordered by publication date. Each entry displays the article title, a two-line excerpt of the body text, and the publication date formatted in Venezuelan locale (`dd/mm/yyyy`).

If no articles have been published yet, the panel shows: _"No hay noticias publicadas."_

## System notices

The right panel shows role-specific alerts under the heading **Avisos de Sistema**. Every user sees a reminder that their module access is limited to their assigned role. Two roles also receive a targeted notice:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Coordinador">
    > ⚠️ Tiene hasta el 15 y el 30 de cada mes para subir los reportes quincenales.

    This reminds coordinators of the biweekly report deadline. Reports not submitted by those dates are considered overdue.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Gestión Humana">
    > 🔔 Hay reportes pendientes por revisar en su bandeja.

    This prompts HR staff to check the **Revisión Reportes** module for submitted reports waiting for review or approval.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Administrator metrics panel

If your role is **Administrador**, a second panel appears below the system notices. It shows four live counters pulled from the database at login:

| Metric | What it counts |
|--------|----------------|
| **Usuarios** | Total registered user accounts |
| **Reportes** | Total biweekly reports submitted across all coordinators |
| **Noticias** | Total news articles in the system (including hidden ones) |
| **Formatos** | Total institutional format documents uploaded |

These counts update each time you reload the dashboard.

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar is your primary navigation tool. It lists only the modules your role can access — links for other roles are never shown.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    On desktop (screens 768 px and wider), the sidebar appears as a fixed left panel. Click the red toggle button on its right edge to collapse it to icon-only view or expand it back to full labels. Your preference persists for the session.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile">
    On mobile, the sidebar is hidden by default. Tap the menu icon in the top navigation bar to open it as a full-screen overlay. Tap outside the sidebar or the **X** button to close it. Tapping any link also closes the menu automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
