The **Noticias** module is where Portal VEN 911's official communications live. Prensa staff and administrators write and publish articles that appear both inside the staff dashboard and on the public-facing landing page. Every authenticated user can read published articles through the dashboard; the public can read visible ones without logging in.

Navigate to **Noticias** in the sidebar at `/dashboard/noticias`.

---

## Who can publish

Only users with the **Prensa** or **Administrador** role see the **Nueva Noticia** button. All other roles can browse and search articles but cannot create or delete them.

The API at `POST /api/news` enforces this restriction — any attempt by a non-Prensa, non-Administrador user returns a `403` error.

---

## Article fields

Each news article has four fields:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Título** | Yes | The article headline, displayed as a heading in both the dashboard and the public landing page |
| **Contenido** | Yes | The body text of the article. Renders as plain text with line breaks preserved |
| **URL de Imagen** | No | A direct URL to an image hosted externally (Imgur, Cloudinary, Google Drive public share, or any direct image URL). A preview renders below the field as you type |
| **Visible** | Yes | Boolean. Controls whether the article appears on the public landing page. Defaults to `true` on creation |

---

## Publishing an article

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the form">
    Click **Nueva Noticia** at the top right of the page. A form panel expands below the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the title and content">
    Fill in **Título** (required) and **Contenido** (required). Both fields must have a value before the form allows submission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an optional image">
    Paste a direct image URL into the **URL de Imagen** field. A live preview renders immediately. If the URL is invalid or the image fails to load, a placeholder is shown instead. Click the **X** button next to the field to clear the image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publicar**. The button shows "Publicando…" while the request is in flight. On success, the form closes, the article list refreshes, and the new article appears at the top.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can use image URLs from Imgur, Flickr, Cloudinary, or any server that returns a direct image file. Google Drive images must be shared publicly and use a direct download link format.
</Tip>

---

## Visibility and the public landing page

The `visible` field determines whether an article appears to unauthenticated visitors on the public landing page. The landing page shows the three most recent articles where `visible = true`.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Administrador">
    The API returns **all** articles regardless of visibility status. Hidden articles show an **Oculta** badge (yellow) next to the date so you can identify them at a glance.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="All other roles">
    The API returns only articles where `visible = true`. Hidden articles are not shown or counted in any view.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Searching articles

A search bar at the top of the article list filters by title in real time. The filter is case-insensitive and matches any substring of the title. Clearing the search field restores the full list.

---

## Deleting an article

Users with the **Prensa** or **Administrador** role see a red trash icon on each article card. Clicking it sends a `DELETE /api/news/{id}` request and immediately removes the article from the list.

<Warning>
  Deleting an article is permanent. There is no archive or recycle bin — once deleted, the article cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

---

## Article metadata

Each article records:

- **author_name** — The name of the user who published it. Displayed at the bottom of the article card as _"Por: [name]"_ when present.
- **published_at** — The timestamp set automatically at creation. Displayed in Venezuelan locale format (`dd/mm/yyyy`).
