Portal VEN 911 is a role-based internal management system for the **Comando Nacional VEN 911** — Venezuela's integrated emergency response network. It provides dedicated workflows for each team: coordinators submit biweekly reports, press staff publish official news, HR reviews submissions, and administrators manage users and system settings. All data lives in a serverless PostgreSQL database and files are stored in Google Drive.

## How the portal works

VEN 911 has two main surfaces: a **public landing page** where citizens can find news and download institutional documents, and a **protected dashboard** where authenticated staff manage operations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in with your credentials">
    Go to the portal login page and enter your institutional email and password. Your administrator will have created your account and assigned your role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your role-specific dashboard">
    After login, you are directed to your personalized dashboard. The sidebar shows only the modules available for your role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Perform your operational tasks">
    Coordinators upload biweekly reports, press staff publish news articles, HR staff review submitted reports, and administrators manage users and view system metrics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download documents anytime">
    Institutional formats and documents are publicly available on the landing page — no login required to download them.
  </Step>
</Steps>
