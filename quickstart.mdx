Portal VEN 911 requires an active account before you can log in. Your administrator creates your account, assigns your role, and provides your credentials. You do not self-register. Once you have your institutional email and password, you can be up and running in under five minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before logging in, confirm you have:

- An active account created by your administrator
- Your institutional email address (format: `usuario@ven911.gob.ve`)
- Your initial password (provided by your administrator)

<Note>
  If you do not know your password or have not received your credentials, contact your system administrator. There is no self-service password reset.
</Note>

## Logging in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the portal">
    Open any modern browser and go to the Portal VEN 911 URL provided by your organization. You will see the public landing page with a login button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the login form">
    Click the login button or navigate directly to `/login`. You will see the **Portal VEN 911 — Acceso al Sistema** form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type your institutional email in the **Correo Electrónico** field (for example, `jperez@ven911.gob.ve`) and your password in the **Contraseña** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Ingresar">
    Click the **Ingresar** button. If your credentials are valid and your account is active, you are redirected immediately to `/dashboard`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  The **Ingresar** button is disabled until both fields contain a value. If you see an error message after clicking, double-check for extra spaces in your email or password.
</Warning>

## Your dashboard

After logging in, the dashboard greets you by name and displays your current role. Every user sees:

- **Recent news** — A feed of the latest official news articles published on the portal
- **System notices** — Role-specific alerts, such as upcoming report deadlines for coordinators or pending review reminders for HR staff

Administrators additionally see **System Metrics**: live counts of total users, submitted reports, published news articles, and available formats.

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar on the left shows only the modules your role can access. Here is what each role sees:

| Module | Coordinador | Prensa | Gestión Humana | Administrador |
|--------|:-----------:|:------:|:--------------:|:-------------:|
| **Inicio** (dashboard home) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Noticias** (news) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Formatos** (institutional documents) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Subir Reportes** (upload reports) | ✓ | — | — | — |
| **Revisión Reportes** (review reports) | — | — | ✓ | — |
| **Usuarios** (user management) | — | — | — | ✓ |
| **Bitacora** (activity log) | — | — | — | ✓ |

## Your first task

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Coordinador">
    Your primary responsibility is submitting biweekly operational reports by the 15th and 30th of each month.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Subir Reportes">
        Click **Subir Reportes** in the sidebar. This module is only visible to coordinators.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Prepare your report file">
        Have your report ready as a PDF or Excel file before uploading. Ensure it covers the correct biweekly period.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload your report">
        Use the upload form to select your file and submit it. The system saves the report and makes it available to Gestión Humana for review.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      The dashboard home displays a reminder about your upcoming report deadline. Check it on login to stay on schedule.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Prensa">
    Your primary responsibility is publishing official news articles that appear on the public portal.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Noticias">
        Click **Noticias** in the sidebar to open the news management module.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create a new article">
        Use the new article form to enter a title and body content for your news item.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish the article">
        Submit the form to publish. The article appears immediately on the public-facing portal and in the dashboard news feed for all staff.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Gestión Humana">
    Your primary responsibility is reviewing coordinator reports as they are submitted.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Revisión Reportes">
        Click **Revisión Reportes** in the sidebar. This module shows all submitted reports from all coordinators.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open a pending report">
        Select a report marked as pending to view its details and the uploaded file.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Mark the report as reviewed">
        Use the review action to mark the report as reviewed. You can also add a comment before confirming.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      The dashboard home displays a reminder when there are reports pending your review.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Administrador">
    Your first task is typically creating accounts for the rest of your team.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Usuarios">
        Click **Usuarios** in the sidebar to open the user management module.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create a new user">
        Fill in the user's name, institutional email, password, and select their role (Coordinador, Prensa, Gestión Humana, or Administrador).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save the account">
        Submit the form. The user can now log in with the credentials you set. Share their email and password with them through a secure channel.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      Administrators see live system metrics on the dashboard home: total user count, report count, news article count, and format count.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
